What is MicroDegree Scholarship?

It's an initiative to support continuous learning of online courses on MicroDegree platform for deserving candidates. Here the course fee is completely sponsored by both kind-hearted donors and MicroDegree family as a 'pay it forward' initiative to empower careers of high-performers, needy and Divyang learners

Criteria for Scholarship? | ವಿದ್ಯಾರ್ಥಿವೇತನಕ್ಕೆ ಮಾನದಂಡ?

  • Candidates who cannot afford the course fees but have an interest in learning Or Divyang Candidates.
  • Candidates need to complete the enrolled course in 30 days for Certification.
